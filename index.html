OPPORTUNITIES AND SUSTAINABLE PROJECTS FOR RURAL POPULATIONS.

ABOUT US

The Mumbo foundation is a charity that seeks to address rural poverty in Kenya. We aim to raise awareness about the plight of women and children living in poverty ravaged rural areas ; and to raise money to support projects to meet their needs. We are conscious of the importance of tight governance, so Trustees visit all our largest projects bi-annually; we require annual budgets and regular reporting throughout the year; we have a local representative who works with the projects throughout the year. This enables us to use all your money on our projects, which directly help the families who really need it.

Life Skills Centre

Nyahera Girls secondary is a school in a small village on the outskirts of the vibrant town of Oyugis in Nyanza Province, Kenya. It is the location of the Mumbo Development Foundation funded Life Skills Centre.

Womens cooperative Enterprises

We have recently committed to funding a women's cooperative society (Chama). Located in Oyugis, Ringa and Kodera. We will build and train local women in value addition of farm produce with the ultimate goal of creating a brand that can be sold in shops and supermarkets thus creating an income from farm produce that typically goes to waste.

Community Health Mobile Clinic

A fitted mobile clinic will operate in the local area with Kenyan and UK volunteers conducting free medical camps within HomaBay county.
